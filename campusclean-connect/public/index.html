Platform features

Everything you need to book and manage campus cleans

From the first request to the final rating — built for students, lecturers, cleaners, and administrators.

🔍

Find & filter cleaners

Search by name, skill, and real-time availability. Request any cleaner or broadcast to whoever's free.

📅

Scheduled bookings

Pick a preferred date and time, mark jobs urgent, and pre-fill your room or office from your profile.

💬

In-app messaging

Every booking gets its own chat thread. Confirm details without exchanging phone numbers.

📍

Live GPS tracking

When a cleaner is on the way, watch their position update on an OpenStreetMap view in real time.

⭐

Ratings & reputation

Rate completed jobs with stars and comments. Cleaners build a public track record that helps everyone choose well.

🎒

Student & lecturer roles

Dorm rooms for students, office locations for lecturers — same powerful booking dashboard for both.

🧹

Cleaner workspace

Available, active, and history tabs. Toggle availability, share location, and manage your profile.

🛡️

Admin oversight

Manage users, filter bookings, handle feedback, and view platform-wide statistics.

Simple workflow

Four steps from request to rating

1

Request

Choose a service, location, building, and time. Pick a cleaner or leave it open.

2

Match

A cleaner accepts your job. You get notified instantly via the platform.

3

Track

Chat to confirm details and follow live location as they head over.

4

Rate

Once marked complete, leave a star rating to help the next person choose.
