The flow

From request to rating in four steps

01

Request

Pick a service, your room, and a time — or aim it at a specific cleaner you've used before.

02

Match

An available cleaner accepts the job, or you're notified the moment your chosen cleaner does.

03

Track

Chat to confirm details and follow your cleaner's live location as they head over.

04

Rate

Once it's marked done, leave a star rating and comment to help the next student choose well.

Three dashboards

One platform, built around each role

🎒

Students

Request cleans, browse and message cleaners, track them live, and rate finished jobs.

  • Search cleaners by rating & skill
  • Open or direct booking requests
  • Live tracking map per booking
  • In-app chat and ratings
🧹

Cleaners

Toggle your availability, accept open jobs, manage active work, and build your reputation.

  • Available / Active / History tabs
  • One-tap live location sharing
  • Direct chat with each student
  • Review history & average rating
🛡️

Admins

Oversee the whole platform: users, bookings, and incoming feedback, all from one screen.

  • Platform-wide stats at a glance
  • Suspend or reactivate accounts
  • Step in on any booking
  • Feedback inbox with status tracking
